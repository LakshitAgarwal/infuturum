ENHANCE YOUR SKILLS AS A MEMBER OF ACM

The Association for Computing Machinery is a US-based international learned society for computing. It was founded in 1947 and is the world's largest scientific and educational computing society. The ACM is a non-profit professional membership group, reporting nearly 110,000 student and professional members as of 2022.

WHAT IS INFUTURUM?

Tighten up your seat belts for INFUTURUM, an enthralling celebration commemorating the 76th anniversary of ACM! Undertake on a mesmerizing voyage that is certain to captivate your intellect and forge an enduring imprint on your memory. Immerse yourself in a diverse array of engaging activities that will not only challenge and exhilarate you but also bestow upon you unparalleled rewards. Vie for breathtaking prizes and become an integral part of a gala that vows to redefine your celebratory experiences. Do not let slip this unparalleled opportunity to revel in opulence and grace, as you become a participant in INFUTURUM—an event destined to be etched in the annals of time for generations to come!
