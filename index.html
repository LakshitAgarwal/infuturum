ENHANCE YOUR SKILLS AS A MEMBER OF ACM

The Association for Computing Machinery is a US-based international learned society for computing. It was founded in 1947 and is the world's largest scientific and educational computing society. The ACM is a non-profit professional membership group, reporting nearly 110,000 student and professional members as of 2022.

WHAT IS INFUTURUM?

Tighten up your seat belts for INFUTURUM, an enthralling celebration commemorating the 76th anniversary of ACM! Undertake on a mesmerizing voyage that is certain to captivate your intellect and forge an enduring imprint on your memory. Immerse yourself in a diverse array of engaging activities that will not only challenge and exhilarate you but also bestow upon you unparalleled rewards. Vie for breathtaking prizes and become an integral part of a gala that vows to redefine your celebratory experiences. Do not let slip this unparalleled opportunity to revel in opulence and grace, as you become a participant in INFUTURUM—an event destined to be etched in the annals of time for generations to come!

What Events
are we hosting?

RESEM

Dive into the intellectual excitement of the Research-Based Hackathon—an intense 26-hour affair for researchers and innovators. Craft a condensed opus, compete in spirited challenges, and forge collaborations to turn ideas into reality. Ride the waves of innovation in this celestial convergence of minds and win exciting prizes.

FishHunt

Get ready for an epic adventure with the Fish Hunt! Solve coding questions, crack secret codes, and tackle challenging puzzles as you race against time to find the hidden treasure on the University campus. It's the perfect opportunity to showcase your problem-solving skills and have a blast with your team! So, are you up for the challenge? Let the Fish Hunt begin!

Hackauction

Groups A and B compete in a coding showdown. Group A crafts websites in Java, CSS, HTML, then faces off with B in an epic framework battle. Winning teams vie for recognition in the ultimate showdown. Ignite your competitive spirit—let the battle begin!

Project Showcase

Get onboard with an extraordinary journey at the Project Showcase—an event where programming brilliance shines. Compete for champion status, present groundbreaking projects, and revel in the joyness of victory. Engage with skilled programmers, make connections, and join the yatch of innovation and excellence.
